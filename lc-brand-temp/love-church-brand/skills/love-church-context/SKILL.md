## Mission, Vision, Method

- **Motivation:** For people to experience God's best for their lives.
- **Mission:** For people to love God supremely and love people supernaturally.
- **Method — the 5S Life:**
  - **Surrendered** — Daily surrendered to God's best
  - **Surrounded** — Growing alongside other believers
  - **Spirit-Led** — Filled to overflowing with God's Spirit
  - **Self-Fed** — Intimacy with God through His Word
  - **Sent** — Constantly looking for the lost

The 5S Life is the operating language of the house. Use the five words exactly when describing the discipleship pathway.

## Doctrinal posture (high level)

- Bible-teaching, Spirit-filled. Affirms gifts of the Spirit (non-cessationist) operated in order.
- Soft-complementarian on women in ministry.
- Default Bible translation: **NKJV** for doctrinal/foundational content. NIV/NLT acceptable for outreach/first-time-guest copy where readability matters; cite translation every time.
- Two ordinances: baptism by immersion + the Lord's Supper (open to all believers).
- "Neither Five-Point Calvinists nor Arminians" — sovereignty + responsibility held together.
